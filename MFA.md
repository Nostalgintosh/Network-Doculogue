# Two-Factor Authentication is a Must

### The Problem
One of the biggest security issues people face, beyond phishing emails, is that no one configures Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

### The Reason
Why? Because most people dislike the extra steps during sign-up. Unfortunately, most people prioritize convenience over security until it is too late.
Don't be that person—set up two-factor authentication.

### What Should I Use?
For most people, the best options are **Microsoft Authenticator** or **Google Authenticator** (sometimes Google is your only option).
You should use a key either physically (hardware key) or store it in a password manager like **Proton Pass.**
